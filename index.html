Those two nights alone pulled mean onset to 3:26am and mean wake to 10:58am, while every other night woke between 9:37 and 10:03am. The fix is unchanged: protect onset, especially on the weekend.
Recovery dipped with the short nights but did not collapse. Readiness averaged 76 and bottomed at 55 on Jun 20. REM averaged 92 min, just over target but down from 107 as the two short nights cut REM to 50 and 33 min. Get to bed earlier and both numbers follow.
