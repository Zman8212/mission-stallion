The one-line version

One clear win (fitness doubled and beat budget), one real improvement (daily upkeep down ~9h), and two levers that did not move at all from Week 1 (money and meditation). Bedtime is still past 1am.

Finding 1: Fitness is the win, it doubled and hit budget

Fitness went 7.6h to ~14.25h, from 54% of budget to 102%. Sunday alone was a movement marathon: padel 1 to 2:30, then 4 hours of beach volleyball, plus biking. A ~5km run Saturday and three gym sessions filled the rest. The only caution: it is weekend-clustered, not spread across the week.

Finding 2: We retired "life maintenance," and upkeep came down ~9h

The lump is gone. The ~20h of daily upkeep is now six real categories: grooming ~7h, eating ~4.5h, laundry ~2.5h, errands ~2.5h, health appts ~2h, cleaning ~1.5h. Two takeaways fall straight out of the split: grooming is the biggest and cannot be outsourced, while chores (laundry + cleaning, ~4h) are the real outsourcing target. Total upkeep is down from 29h in Week 1, the most encouraging structural move in the data, if it holds.

Finding 3: Money and meditation did not move

Money is ~12h, still 48% of budget, still zero on the weekend, still shoved into late-afternoon fragments (Wednesday's block ran 5 to 6:45pm after the whole day). Meditation is ~3.5h against a 14h target, several days at zero. These are the same two misses as Week 1, unchanged.

Finding 4: Leaks are flat but shifted to sports-watching

Leaks held at ~10.5h (Week 1 was 10.3h), but the mix moved. Sports-watching is the new big one: World Cup Tuesday (~3h), soccer Wednesday and Thursday, ~5.5h in total. Instagram-on-waking is still nearly daily; weed on the weekend nights. Note that reading is no longer counted here, it got promoted to its own bucket (~3.5h) because it is genuinely good for you, not dead time.

Bedtime is still the unbuilt habit

Onsets stayed past 1am all week; last night went down at 12:52am and slept 9.1h but only by waking at 11:04am, so it was catch-up sleep, not an earlier bedtime. The Week 1 finding held: you sleep fine once you are down, you just do not get down before 1am.

Progress on last week's three levers

  • Outsource the chores: partial. Upkeep down ~9h, and you were pricing a cleaning setup Friday, but nothing is standing yet.
  • Hard bedtime anchor: not done. Still 1am-plus onsets every night.
  • Protect one daylight money block: not done. Money work is still fragmented and pushed past 5pm.

Two changes we made to the system this week

  • Killed the "life maintenance" bucket and itemized it into grooming, eating, laundry, errands, health, cleaning, so we can see and target each one.
  • Reading is its own bucket now (not a leak), and Dan's class counts as Bachata.

For the coach: two things to settle

  • Re-budget the upkeep split. The old 7h life-maintenance target now needs to become per-category targets. Grooming alone ran ~7h, so 7h total was never realistic.
  • Set a Reading target and, if we want it, carve it out of the budget (which is currently maxed at 168h with no slack).
